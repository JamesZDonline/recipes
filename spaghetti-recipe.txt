Ingredients
-1/2 teaspoon of Basil
-1/2 teaspoon of Sage
-1/4 teaspoon of Thyme
-1/4 teaspoon of Cayanne Pepper
-1 can of tomato paste
-2 cans of tomato soup
-1 package of ground italian sweet sausage
-1 large onion

Directions:
1. Dice onion
2. Brown onion and sausage in pan
3. Add tomato soup/paste and seasoning
4. Simmer uncovered for an hour
5. Make noodles
6. Enjoy
